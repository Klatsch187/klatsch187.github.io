The typing feel is fantastic — tactile, responsive, satisfying. The problem is
everyone on my calls can hear every keystroke like popcorn. I swapped in quieter
switches, which helped, but it's still a conversation piece for the wrong
reasons. Good keyboard, questionable for shared spaces.
